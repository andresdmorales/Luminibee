Our Mission

The goal of Luxbi, Inc. is to host a datadbase of e-learning tools for science and technology that is open to everyone. This will increase the community drive to learn.

  • We collect and sort e-learning tools used for science and technology. We hope to compile them in a database where they are openly accessible.

  • We aim to build a community of: science and technology professionls, students, and learners. They will be able to contribute to and benefit from this platform.

  • As we do the aforementioned, we hope to develop new e-lerning tools unique to Luxbi.
